üí° Why HEIC to PNG?

Apple introduced the HEIC format with iOS 11 to save storage space while maintaining image quality. While HEIC is excellent for iOS devices, it's not widely supported on Windows, Android, or the web. Converting to PNG ensures:

  • Universal compatibility across all devices and platforms
  • Easy sharing via email, messaging apps, and social media
  • Ability to upload to websites and services that don't support HEIC
  • No quality loss during conversion
